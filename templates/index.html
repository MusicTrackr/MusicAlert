What is MusicTrackr?

MusicTrackr is a great way to Never miss an album again. It was built at the hackBCA 24-hour hackathon with a simple mission in mind; keep fans up-to-date with their favourite artists' new releases. Powered by iTunes and SendGrid, simply enter your e-mail address and the name of the artist whose releases you'd like to subscribe to and you're off. You'll never be a month late to today's music again.
